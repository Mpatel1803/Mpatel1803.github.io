Frequently Asked Questions

Having issues? Look into our Frequently Asked Questions and see if you get your solution

  • Do not know which program best fits you?

    If you do not know which program suites you best, you can explore the different courses and even reach out to our student advising team on their email - student.advisor@scholarship.com

  • Program of choice not in list?

    If the program you want to do is not in our list, do not be disappointed. Reach out to us on support@scholarship.com and inform us about the program you wish to take. Our support team will try make arrangements to support you.

  • Facing issues while logging in?

    If you cannot access your account because you forgot the password, do not worry. We have you coverd! Use the 'Forgot Password' option and reset it at your own conviniece.
